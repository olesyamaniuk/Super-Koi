About the Game

Super Koi – Build a Fish Pond lets you design and manage your very own koi sanctuary. From humble beginnings to a lush paradise, grow your pond step by step. Breed unique koi, arrange beautiful aquatic plants, and customize your landscape for the ultimate zen experience. It’s more than a game—it’s a peaceful retreat in your pocket.

FAQ

  • Is Super Koi free to play?
    Yes, the game is free with optional in-app purchases.
  • Can I play offline?
    Absolutely! You can enjoy your pond even without an internet connection.
  • How many koi types are there?
    There are over 50 unique koi to discover and breed.
  • Is there a limit to pond size?
    You can expand your pond over time as you progress in the game.
  • Can I move and redesign my pond later?
    Yes! Everything is customizable—change layouts anytime.
